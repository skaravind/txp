What is TED?

TED is a nonprofit organization devoted to Ideas Worth Spreading. Started as a four-day conference in California 33 years ago, TED has grown to support its mission with multiple initiatives. The two annual TED Conferences invite the world’s leading thinkers and doers to speak for 18 minutes or less. Many of these talks are then made available, free, at TED.com. TED speakers have included Bill Gates, Jane Goodall, Elizabeth Gilbert, Sir Richard Branson, Nandan Nilekani, Philippe Starck, Ngozi Okonjo-Iweala, Sal Khan, Daniel Kahneman, Elon Musk, Shahrukh Khan and many more. TED’s media initiatives include TED.com, where new TED Talks are posted daily.

What is TEDx?

Faced with its success, TED wanted to allow the broader community of its fans to spread the TED spirit around the world. The TEDx program was created for this: continue to share ideas and our passion for knowledge. "X" means events that are organized independently while meeting a number of criteria defined by EFILE.

About TEDx, x = independently organized event

In the spirit of ideas worth spreading, TEDx is a program of local, self-organized events that bring people together to a TED-like experience. At a TEDx event, TEDTalks video and live speakers combine to spark deep discussion and connection in a small group. These local, self-organized events are branded TEDx, where x = independently organized.

What is TEDx Pandri?

We are a community of budding entrepreneurs, radical thinkers, doers and those consistently striving for change. This is an ever growing community set out to tap out the immense potential, our Chhattisgarh holds in context to entrepreneurial ventures and initiatives. Our community is multi spectral congregation of artists, businessmans, innovators, freelancers, Startups, technopreneurs hailing out of diverse backgrounds from and around Chhattisgarh.
